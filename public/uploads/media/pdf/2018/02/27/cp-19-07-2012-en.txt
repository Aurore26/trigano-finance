<p class="exergue">Finalization of the acquisition of Gaupen Henger</p>
<p>After receiving the approval of the Norwegian  competition authority for the acquisition of 100% of the capital of Gaupen  Henger, the leader in the manufacturing of trailers in Norway, Trigano confirms  the finalization of this acquisition.</p>
<p>Gaupen Henger employs 30 people  and recorded 93 million Norwegian Crown sales in 2011 (12.5 million Euros). This acquisition, together with that  of Lider in France on 6 July 2012, allows Trigano to assert its number 1  position in Europe with sales of 100 million euros and a market share of more  than 15%.</p>
